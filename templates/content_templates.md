# Content Templates

## Teardown Format

- What I looked at
- What works
- What feels weak or inconsistent
- Why that matters commercially
- What I would fix first
- Closing observation

## Case Study Format

- Context
- Problem
- Constraints
- Approach
- System decisions
- Before and after shift
- Business effect
- What this means for similar teams

## Insight Post Format

- Sharp observation
- Why people miss it
- What it affects in the business
- Practical takeaway
- Closing line or CTA

## Before/After Format

- Before state
- Hidden cost of the old setup
- Structural change made
- After state
- Why the change held up
